POCKET 
PAYMENT SYSTEM DESIGN
Ecommerce & Delivery Marketplace
1. Objective
To implement a secure and compliant payment system for a marketplace application that connects Buyers, Sellers, and Delivery Agents — without holding customer funds, operating a wallet system, or falling under financial regulatory classification.
2. Core Principle
The platform acts as a Marketplace Orchestrator — not a financial intermediary. All funds are collected, held, and disbursed exclusively by PawaPay, a licensed payment provider.
3. 
POCKET 
Payment Flow
Step 1 — Order Placement
Buyer selects a product from the marketplace
Delivery fee is calculated (fixed or distance-based)
Total amount = Product price + Delivery fee
Step 2 — Payment Collection
Buyer pays via Mobile Money (MTN / Airtel
/Zamtel
)
Payment is processed by PawaPay; funds move into a holding account managed by the gateway
Step 3 — Order Processing
Seller receives the order and prepares the product
A delivery agent is assigned  (bidding
 depending who can afford to pick
)
Step 4 — Delivery & Confirmation
Delivery agent confirms delivery via the app
(by the buyer scanning the qr code for delivery app to confrim handoff)
Buyer confirmation is optional but recommended
Step 5 — Payout Execution
After delivery confirmation, the system triggers payouts via the PawaPay API:
Seller receives the product amount
Delivery agent receives the delivery fee
Platform retains a commission (optional)
4. System Scope
What the system WILL do
What the system will NOT do
Track orders and transactions
Calculate earnings per transaction
Trigger payouts after service completion
Provide read-only earnings visibility
Store or hold user money
Provide wallet functionality
Maintain withdrawable balances
Delay payouts arbitrarily
5. Earnings Display (UI)
Users (Sellers and Delivery Agents) will have read-only visibility into their earnings:
Total earnings to date
Earnings broken down per order
Completed transactions history
Pending payouts (not yet processed)
Note: The UI will not display wallet balance, available balance, or any withdraw-on-demand functionality.
6. Security & Verification
Payment confirmation is received via gateway webhooks from PawaPay
Backend verifies payment status before marking any order as paid
Frontend payment status is never trusted — all verification is server-side
7. Optional Future Enhancements
Automated payout scheduling
Refund handling via the PawaPay gateway
Delivery tracking integration (maps)
Commission optimization and reporting dashboard
Summary
This system ensures that:
•  The platform never holds or controls money
•  All funds are managed exclusively by PawaPay
•  Payments are transaction-based, not balance-based
•  The platform remains a pure ecommerce marketplace
