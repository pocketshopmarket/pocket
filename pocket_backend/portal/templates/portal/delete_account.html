Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your My PocketShop account and personal data

How it works

  • Enter the mobile number registered to your Pocket Shop account below.
  • Our team will contact you on that number to verify that you own the account. This protects your account from being deleted by someone else.
  • Once verified, your account is deleted within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account, profile details (name, phone number, addresses), saved payment methods, wishlist, and notifications.
  • Product listings and shop details, if you are a seller.

What we keep

  • Completed order and payment records are retained as required by Zambian financial regulations, but they are no longer linked to an active account.
