How It Works.

From order to payout in four simple steps. No complexity, no surprises.

1

Order

Buyer selects a product and pays via mobile money. Funds are held securely by the payment gateway.

2

Prepare

Seller receives the order notification and prepares the product. A delivery agent is matched.

3

Deliver

Rider picks up the order and delivers it. Buyer scans the QR code to confirm handoff.

4

Payout

Seller gets the product price, rider gets the delivery fee — instantly to their mobile money.
