1. About Pocket Shop

Pocket Shop ("we", "us", "our") is an online marketplace that connects buyers, sellers, and delivery agents across Zambia. By creating an account or using our platform at mypocketshop.store or the Pocket Shop mobile application, you agree to these Terms of Service.

2. Who Can Use Pocket Shop

You must be at least 18 years old to create an account. By registering, you confirm that all information you provide — including your phone number, full name, and identity documents — is accurate and belongs to you. Creating fake accounts or impersonating another person is a serious violation and will result in immediate suspension.

3. Your Account

  • You register using your Zambian mobile phone number. A one-time PIN (OTP) is sent by SMS to verify that the number belongs to you.
  • You are responsible for keeping your account secure. Never share your OTP or session with anyone.
  • We may suspend or terminate your account if we detect fraudulent activity, false identity information, or violations of these Terms.

4. Buyers

  • You may browse and purchase products from verified sellers on the platform.
  • Payment is collected at checkout through mobile money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, or Zamtel Kwacha).
  • Orders can be fulfilled by delivery to your address or by pickup directly from the seller's shop.
  • If a seller does not accept your order within the configured time window, your order is automatically cancelled and a full refund is initiated.
  • If you cancel an order before the seller begins preparing it, you are entitled to a full refund.

5. Sellers

  • To list products, you must complete seller verification. Tier 1 requires your NRC (National Registration Card) and a live verification selfie. Tier 2 requires your business registration documents.
  • You are responsible for the accuracy of your listings — including price, product description, stock availability, and images.
  • When a buyer places an order, you must accept or reject it within the time shown in the app. Failure to act will trigger automatic cancellation and refund to the buyer.
  • Pocket Shop charges a platform commission on each completed order. The current rate is displayed in your seller dashboard. We may adjust the rate with advance notice.
  • You must not list counterfeit, stolen, illegal, or prohibited goods.

Seller Payouts: Your payout (product total minus platform commission) is sent to your registered mobile money wallet when the delivery agent scans the pickup QR code at your shop — confirming they have collected the order. Payouts are processed manually by our team within 24 hours of pickup confirmation.

6. Delivery Agents

  • To deliver orders, you must complete delivery agent verification, including your driver's license and a live selfie.
  • You are responsible for safe, prompt delivery. You must scan the QR code at the seller's location (pickup) and at the buyer's location (drop-off) to confirm each handoff.
  • During an active delivery, your real-time GPS location is shared with the buyer and seller for tracking purposes.

Rider Payouts: Your delivery fee is sent to your registered mobile money wallet when you scan the drop-off QR code at the buyer's location, confirming successful delivery. Payouts are processed manually by our team within 24 hours of drop-off confirmation.

7. Payments and Refunds

  • All transactions are processed in Zambian Kwacha (ZMW) through PawaPay, a licensed mobile money payment gateway.
  • Pocket Shop does not store your mobile money PIN or password at any point.
  • Refunds are initiated within 24 hours of a qualifying cancellation. The time for funds to appear in your wallet depends on your mobile money provider (typically within a few minutes to a few hours).
  • In the event of a payment dispute, contact us at pocketstore@gmail.com. We aim to investigate and respond within 5 business days.
